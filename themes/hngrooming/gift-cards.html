Frequently Asked Questions

When does my gift card expire?

Gift cards never expire so you can use them anytime.

Can I use my gift card online?

Absolutely! You can book any service through our app or online webstore with a Hammer & Nails gift card.

What purchases can I use my gift card towards?

You can purchase any services or retail with your Hammer & Nails gift card.

Can I regift my gift card to someone else?

For in-store gift card purchases, you can physically gift that gift card to anyone else. If you have the gift card number, anyone can redeem a gift card and is not attached to the recipient's email.

Where can I purchase a gift card?

Gift cards can be purchased through our app, online webstore, or in-store. Visit our locations page to find your nearest location.

Can I use my gift card at any location?

Gift cards can be used at any of our Hammer & Nails franchise locations.

How much should I put on a gift card to make a complete gift?

Check out our service menu for offerings and decide what is the best experience your recipient to have. Prices vary by location. Visit our locations page to find your nearest location.

Can gratuity be added to a gift card?

Yes, you can include the gratuity amount in your gift card purchase.

Is there a minimum value I can put on a gift card?

There is no minimum required to purchase a gift card.
